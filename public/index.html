Google Colab Free GPU Setup

Generate your videos for free by connecting to Google Colab's cloud T4 GPU. Follow these instructions carefully:

Download Colab Script (colab_tunnel.py)
1.

Open [Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com) in your browser and log in.

2.

Create a new notebook, upload the downloaded colab_tunnel.py script, and mount Google Drive when prompted (necessary for storing models and outputs).

3.

Run the script. Note: It will take approximately 20-25 minutes to download all checkpoints and initialize the tunnel.

4.

The script will output a secure public URL (e.g. https://xxxx.trycloudflare.com).

5.

Copy that URL, go to the Create Video studio, select ComfyUI under Cloud Provider, and paste it into the tunnel URL box.

⚠️

Keep the Colab page open and running in your browser tab while you generate videos.
