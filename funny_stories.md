# 🎬 50 Funny Children's Stories for AI Video Studio

Welcome! This file contains **50 funny, highly visual, and short stories** perfect for creating YouTube Shorts (9:16) or widescreen videos. 
Each story has:
1. **Title and Theme**
2. **Style & Visual Prompts** (to paste into the AI Video Prompt box)
3. **Narration Script** (exactly one sentence per line—simply copy and paste this into the **Video Script** box!).
